About

I am a final-year Master student at NLPGM Lab @ Wuhan University, supervised by Prof. Tieyun Qian. I obtained my B.Eng. with honors from Wuhan University in 2023.

My research focuses on understanding and improving the generalization of AI alignment. Recently, I am particularly interested in leveraging the Platonic Representation Hypothesis as a bridge to enhance generalization in AI alignment across data distributions, modalities, and models.
