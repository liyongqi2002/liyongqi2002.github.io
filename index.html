About

Hi! I am a second-year Master student at NLPGM Lab @ Wuhan University, under the supervision of Prof. Tieyun Qian. I obtained my B.Eng. from Wuhan University in 2023.

My research focuses on aligning AI models with humans by employing techniques inspired by causal reasoning and reinforcement learning.

I am committed to developing trustworthy AI that can communicate effectively with humans.
